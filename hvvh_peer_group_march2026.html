What We Are Solving Today

The Founder Peer Group will deep-dive into these shared hurdles:

    Go-To-Market Balance Weighing immediate direct-to-consumer cash flow against lucrative but lengthy B2B enterprise sales cycles.

    Partnership Activation Moving massive channel partners past the initial introduction phase to become active champions of a product.

    Technical Transitions Keeping product development moving forward while upgrading or swapping technical teams.
