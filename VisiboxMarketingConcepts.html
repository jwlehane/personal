Visibox Marketing Concepts

Bringing the Sphere Experience to Everyone

Veo Video Ad Prompts (3 Seconds)

Prompt 1: The Explosion

A hyper-realistic, cinematic shot of a single laptop on a stage. A finger taps the spacebar, and instantly, the entire background behind it explodes into a massive, 270-degree projection of a vibrant, moving galaxy, enveloping the stage.

Prompt 2: The Merge

A rapid, split-screen sequence. On the left, a musician passionately plays guitar. On the right, a VJ triggers clips. The split-screen merges to show the musician on stage alone, a MIDI foot pedal at their feet, controlling the exact same epic visuals themselves.

Prompt 3: The Transformation

A small, plain white room. A hand places a small projector on a table. When it's turned on, the walls instantly transform into a wrap-around, immersive video of a lush, hyper-realistic jungle with sounds of birds and rustling leaves. The final frame shows the Visibox logo and the tagline: "Your World. Reimagined."
